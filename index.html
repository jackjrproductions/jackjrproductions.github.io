About Jack Jr

Jack is an all-round DJ born in Suriname, raised in Miami and now resides in Belgium, he grew up playing many instruments such as trombone, baritone and percussion. He has been DJing for over 8 years doing private parties, special events, corporate events and was the resident DJ at Archief in Leuven, Belgium where an event was created called American Wednesday. His love for music is pure which translates to a good time for whatever crowd he's performing for. With productions in works there is no doubt that he will be known throughout the world in due time, spreading his life experience through his music to the world.
